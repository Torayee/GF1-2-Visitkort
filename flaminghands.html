Flaming Hands

Flaming hands or Soul, is a project I made on my boarding school (HE).

This flaming soul, is a project I made in my boarding school year (21-22), it's a very touching peice for myself and a picture of how far I have come.

The painting is made by acrylic and primarly used the primal colors- plus a bit of white. There is NO black used in the piece, it's all a mix between yellow, red, dark green, purple, blue. Went for a realistic kind of look, and personally think it worked out pretty alrigth. //^^//
